Accidents in India

India witnesses thousands of road and workplace accidents every year. Lack of awareness, poor safety practices, and negligence are among the top causes. It is essential to follow safety measures to prevent these tragic incidents.

Safety Measures

  • Always wear seat belts and helmets.
  • Follow traffic rules and speed limits.
  • Avoid using mobile phones while driving.
  • Ensure proper workplace safety equipment.
  • Raise awareness and educate others.
